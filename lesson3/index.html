Site Name

The site is going to be called Delmas Chamber .The site's name is DELMAS CHAMBER because it's a portal for all businesses located in the area of Delmas City, which is the largest contributing city in Haiti, according to the report from the National Directorate of Taxes in Haiti."

Site Purpose

The site's purpose is to provide a way for everyone to recognize all business categories in the region, to facilitate connections between businesses and the public based on transparency, and to reduce unethical practices in the city's business community.
